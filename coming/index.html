SleepV is Coming Soon...


This site is dedicated to sleep intraindividual variability (IIV, nightly variation).
In early 2015, we completed a systematic review trying to answer,
"What do we know about sleep IIV? Does it matter?"
We are making this site to continue the search for answers. Here you will find:

  • An ongoing systematic review on the correlates of sleep IIV
  • Information on methodologies in researching IIV
  • Opportunities to contribute to this growing body of literature
